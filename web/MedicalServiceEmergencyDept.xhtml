Emergency Medical Care 24/7

Twenty-four hours a day, seven days a week, 365 days a year, the Milford Hospital Emergency Department is here to help. Our board certified emergency physicians and team of medical professionals are highly trained in emergency care and are ready to provide the best care possible, promptly and efficiently. Our Emergency Department offers:

  • Up-to-date facilities and equipment.
  • Highly skilled emergency medical staff and access to specialists.
  • A convenient patient flow process.
  • Separate entrance for ambulance and walk in patients.
  • Rooms that are equipped for special needs including: pediatrics, orthopaedics, gynecology, psychology, cardiac care and ears, nose and throat.
  • Immediate access to diagnostic imaging 24 hours a day.
