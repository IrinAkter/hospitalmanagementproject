  • At the end of the ramp, turn right and follow blue Hospital signs.

Directions to Milford Hospital Walk In Center

From Hartford Region and Points North

  • Take I-91 South to I-95 South to Exit 39A – Rte 1 South.
  • At the end of the ramp turn right.
  • Keep Right at fork.
  • Milford Hospital Walk In Center will be approximately ½ mile on the left.

From Fairfield County and Points South

  • Take I-95 North to Exit 37 (High Street).
  • Turn right onto High Street.
  • Left onto Boston Post Road (Rte 1).
  • Milford Hospital Walk In Center will be about one mile on the right.

From Merritt/Wilbur Cross Parkways and Points North

  • Take Exit 54 towards I-95 (this is the Milford Parkway) Follow Milford Parkway to the end.
  • Right at end of exit ramp.
  • Milford Hospital Walk In Center is approximately ½ mile on the right.

From Merritt/Wilbur Cross Parkways and Points South

  • Take Exit 54 towards I-95 (this is the Milford Parkway).
  • Follow Milford Parkway to the end.
  • Right at end of exit ramp.
  • Milford Hospital Walk In Center is approximately ½ mile on the right.

Gift Shop

The Enchanted Window Gift Shop, a service of the Milford Hospital Auxiliary, is located in the Main Lobby of Milford Hospital. Tax-free gifts, magazines, candy, cards, flowers and personal items are available for purchase. Phone orders for flowers or gifts to be delivered to our patients are welcome.

Hours of Operation:

Monday – Friday: 9:00 am – 8:00 pm
Saturday – Sunday: 11:00 am – 3:00 pm
Phone Number: 203-876-4059

Additional Amenities

Milford Hospital offers our patients and visitors free wireless internet access. An ATM is located in our main lobby, outside the gift shop.

Cafeteria

The Milford Hospital cafeteria is located on the 2nd floor. It is open 7 days a week and offers hot meal selections, a variety of deli sandwiches, salads and cold options as well as snacks and beverages.

Hot meal options are available as follows:

Breakfast: 7:00am – 9:30am
Lunch: 11:00am – 1:30pm
Dinner: 4:30pm – 6:30pm

Cold meal options

Sandwiches, salads, snacks and beverages are available throughout the day during regular cafeteria hours.

The cafeteria is closed from 10:30 – 11:00am and 1:30 – 2:00pm daily. Vending machines are located outside of the Memorial II and Memorial III West.

Kind Expressions / Send a Patient a Greeting

Kind Expressions is an "e-greeting" service of Milford Hospital. Attended to by our Volunteer Services Department, family and friends can send a free Kind Expression Message to our inpatients. Be sure to include the patients first name, last name and room number or unit if possible. Kind Expressions are delivered on weekdays from 9:00am – 4:30pm.
