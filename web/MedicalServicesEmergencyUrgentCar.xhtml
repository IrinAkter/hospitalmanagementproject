A convenient alternative for
non-emergency cases.

The Milford Hospital Walk In and Urgent Care Center is a convenient alternative to a busy hospital emergency department for prompt medical care of minor illnesses and injuries.

The Milford Hospital Walk In Center is located at:
831 Boston Post Road
Milford, Connecticut
Phone: (203) 876-4101

Hours: Monday – Friday 8:00 am – 7:30 pm
Saturday – Sunday 8:00am – 5:30 pm

Conditions Appropriate for Urgent Care

  • Sore throat, sinus pain, cough, earache or other flu like symptoms
  • Cuts, scrapes, burns and wounds
  • Minor eye infections / pink eye
  • Minor injuries, sprains or strains
  • Urinary pain / bladder infection
  • Other minor conditions that require prompt attention, but do not pose an immediate, serious health threat

For Your Health

Our Walk In Center is not an emergency department. It should be used only for minor conditions that do not pose an immediate, serious health threat.

  • For serious illness or injury, call 911 or go directly to the Milford Hospital Emergency Department.
  • Always call 911 if you are experiencing chest pain, severe abdominal pain, or have sustained multiple injuries. Life-saving care can then begin in your home and continue on your way to the hospital.

Milford Hospital recommends that every individual have a relationship with a primary care physician. If you or your family are looking for a new doctor - internist, family practitioner, gynecologist, or any specialist - please review our Physician Locator on this website or call 203-876-4500 our referral staff will help you select a physician based on location, experience, and other factors important to you.
