Several locations and early hours for
your convenience.

Milford Medical Laboratory (MML), a subsidiary of Milford Hospital, provides comprehensive testing capabilities at our patient service centers in Milford and Orange. Milford Medical Laboratory has modern instrumentation and advanced equipment run by trained and experienced specialists. With early hours, two convenient locations, and no appointment necessary, the laboratory is designed for our patients convenience.

Locations and Hours

Satellite Locations

2068 Bridgeport Ave., Milford (opposite Milford Hospital)

849 Boston Post Rd., Milford Hospital Medical Building

The labs are certified to perform high complexity tests by the Connecticut Department of Health and the Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services under the Clinical Laboratory Improvement Act of 1988.
