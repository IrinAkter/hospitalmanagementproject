A commitment to the community
education and wellness.

Milford Hospital is committed to the health and wellness of our community. Throughout the year, at the hospital and at other locations in the community, we offer prevention programs, support groups, nutrition and exercise sessions, as well as informational programs on holistic health and new treatments for common conditions. Taught by specially trained experts, our programs are open to public and offered at little or no cost.

We believe that education, information and prevention are the best medicine and is key in overall health and well-being. To live well is to be well.
