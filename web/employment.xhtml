Our employees are our most important asset.

Milford Hospital is one of the area's largest employers with more than 800 staff members across a wide range of healthcare, administrative and support disciplines. At Milford Hospital we realize that our most important assets are our employees.

Milford Hospital is located in a safe, suburban neighborhood, centrally located in Southern Connecticut. We are minutes away from I-95 and the Merritt Parkway.

For a listing of all available full-time, part-time and per diem job opportunities please click here.

If you are interested in a particular position, you can download, print and complete a job application in Word or PDF format. You may also pick up an application at the hospital's information desk between 7 a.m. and 8 p.m. daily. Please fax resumes to Human Resources at 203-876-4224, or e-mail us at hr@milfordhospital.org.
