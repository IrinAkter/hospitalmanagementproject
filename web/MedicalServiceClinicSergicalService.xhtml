Top surgeons meet high technology.

Milford Hospital is proud to offer our patients a convenient, safe, comfortable and caring surgical experience. Our SurgiCenter is available to meet the surgical needs of both our inpatients as well as those scheduled for one-day surgical procedures.

Whether your case is routine or medically complex, we offer the most advanced intervention and treatment options. Milford Hospital offers a growing number of state-of-the-art minimally invasive and computer-assisted surgical procedures in our technologically advanced operating rooms.

The outstanding skills and training of our surgeons, the technology we make available to them, and our highly trained and experienced support team of anesthesiologists and nurses all contribute to the patients' overall surgical experience.

Our Surgeons

Many of the highly skilled and experienced surgeons who practice at Milford Hospital have also practiced at large, tertiary-care hospitals. All are board certified. They come to Milford Hospital because of our commitment to providing them with the most advanced tools possible as well as our overall commitment to patient centered care. To find a surgeon, please view our Physician Locator.

The Milford Hospital SurgiCenter and Operating Rooms are equipped for most of your surgical needs including, but not limited to:

  • Joint Replacement
  • Orthopaedics
  • General Surgery
  • Urology
  • Gynecology
  • Podiatry
  • Plastic / Cosmetic Surgery
  • Opthamology
  • Otolaryngology

Preparing for Your Surgery at Milford Hospital

Thank you for choosing Milford Hospital for your surgical experience. Our staff and your surgeon are working together to ensure that your surgical experience will be convenient, safe, comfortable and caring.

This brochure is designed to help answer questions you may have as you prepare for surgery. Further questions can be answered by your surgeon or our staff.
