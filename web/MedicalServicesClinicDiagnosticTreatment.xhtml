Offering excellent care to our outpatients.

The Diagnostic and Treatment Center (DTC) at Milford Hospital is dedicated to providing our patients with a variety of minimally invasive, outpatient therapeutic and diagnostic procedures.

Diagnostic and Treatment Center services include:

  • A complete range of GI (Gastrointestinal) procedures including endoscopy and colonoscopy
  • Interventional Radiological procedures
  • IV Infusion Therapy
  • Chemotherapy
  • Blood products
  • Antibiotic therapy
  • Pain management

Most patients undergoing procedures in the Diagnostic and Treatment Center will have the convenience of private rooms with a dedicated Registered Nurse attending to their needs. Our facility is equipped with High Definition Monitoring equipment enabling physicians to diagnose and treat GI diseases and other conditions. A highly trained and experienced support team of anesthesiologists and nurses will all contribute to the patients' overall care and comfort.

For more information please call the Diagnostic and Treatment Center at 203.876.4650.
